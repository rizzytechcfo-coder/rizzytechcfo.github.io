About

I’m a Master of Professional Accountancy (MPAc) candidate at UC San Diego with a background in electrical engineering. I focus on building smart, scalable solutions at the intersection of accounting, finance, and analytics.

Earlier hands-on work in operations and systems thinking shaped how I now approach financial analysis and process improvement. Today I combine accounting depth with technical tools—Python, Jupyter, and statistical/ML methods—to support financial modeling, valuation, forecasting, controls, and automation. Recent work includes regression and classification workflows, ensemble methods, and scripted extraction of information from SEC filings to accelerate analysis.

My goal is to deliver practical, data-driven insights by pairing traditional accounting principles with modern analytical techniques. This site serves as a portfolio to demonstrate how engineering rigor can strengthen finance, valuation, and decision support.

Note: This GitHub Pages site is a non-commercial showcase of skills and education.
